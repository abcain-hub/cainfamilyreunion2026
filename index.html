Address: 13 Rodeo Lane · Choteau, MT 59422

About

Experience an authentic Montana 4th of July rodeo! The Choteau American Legion puts on one of the finest small-town Independence Day rodeos in Big Sky Country — complete with bull riding, barrel racing, roping, and all the western festivities that make the holiday special. A perfect way to celebrate the 4th of July in true Montana style!

Off-Site · July 5 · 11:00 AM

🥾 Guided Sluice Boxes Hike

Group: 11 hikers · Guided

Includes: Picnic lunch to go

About Sluice Boxes State Park

Sluice Boxes State Park sits along Belt Creek canyon near Monarch, Montana — a dramatic landscape of limestone cliffs, waterfalls, and rugged terrain carved by Belt Creek. The park takes its name from the gold mining operations of the 1800s. A guided hike brings you through this stunning canyon with incredible views of Montana's Rocky Mountain Front. Don't forget your camera!
